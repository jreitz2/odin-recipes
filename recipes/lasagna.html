Lasagna

Description

Baked Italian dish consisting of wide strips of pasta cooked and layered with meat or vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Marinara sauce
  • Shredded cheese
  • Ricotta
  • Ground beef

Directions

  1. Brown the ground beef
  2. Par boil the noodles
  3. Layer all ingredients appropriately
  4. Bake in over for 45 minutes at 350 degrees
